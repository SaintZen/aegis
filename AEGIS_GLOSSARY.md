# AEGIS GLOSSARY

Unified Terminology for Somatic Stabilization — V1.0

## A

### Anchor (Monolith Anchor)

A central grounding mass on the Bridge.  
Used for compression holds, Kill Switch activation, and somatic stabilization.

### Anchor Hold

A sustained press on the Anchor that applies mechanical pressure to reduce subsurface drift.

## B

### Bridge

The operational surface of the Aegis system.  
Cold, symmetrical, and stable.  
The user's reference point during destabilization.

## C

### Containment (Vault Containment)

The act of storing a signal in the Vault without review or rumination.

### Crazy Feet

Metaphor for subsurface turbulence—rapid, pre-cognitive internal motion beneath a calm exterior.

## D

### Depth Index

The geological record of system activity.  
Logs Purge Events, Hollow activations, Anchor holds, Vault deposits, and uptime.

### Drop (Kill Switch Drop)

The moment the system transitions from Bridge to Void after Kill Switch activation.

## E

### Energy, Subsurface

Internal pressure detected before conscious awareness.  
Measured indirectly through system events.

## F

### Freeze Lockout

Temporary UI suspension during Kill Switch activation to prevent interference.

## G

### Gravitational Shredding

The 2.5-second collapse animation in the Void where a thought bubble spirals into singularity.

### Ground Plane

The user's vertical orientation reference.  
Recalibrated through somatic protocols.

## H

### Haptic Escalation

A structured vibration sequence: Light -> Medium -> Heavy -> Snap.  
Used during Purge Events.

### Hollow

The 7th Sense interface.  
A presence, not a tool.  
Activates when pre-cognitive signals are detected.

### Hollow Pulse

The 6-second breathing ring cycle that synchronizes with somatic rhythm.

## I

### Integrity Stabilized

System confirmation message after a successful purge.  
Indicates subsurface energy has been discharged.

## K

### Kill Switch

Somatic Override Pathway.  
Long press on Anchor -> immediate Void entry -> forced purge.

## L

### Lab (Diagnostic Arc)

Subsystem that measures subsurface signals through four orbs: Somatic, Atmospheric, Hollow, Vault.

### Ledger (Technical Ledger)

Cold, monospaced audit log of system events.  
Exports via Direct Print Stream.

## M

### Monolith

The visual and functional representation of the Anchor.  
A digital mass.

## P

### Purge Event

A complete Void cycle resulting in signal discharge.

### Presence (Hollow Presence)

The Hollow's non-interactive state of awareness during signal detection.

## R

### Ratio, Stability

A mechanical relationship:

(Anchor Holds + Hollow Activations) / Purge Events

Not interpretive.  
Not emotional.  
Purely structural.

## S

### Signal

A pre-cognitive somatic spike.  
Treated as data, not emotion.

### Signal Flow

The movement of a signal through the system:  
Detection -> Hollow -> Vault/Void -> Ledger -> Bridge.

### Snap

The final haptic event in the Purge sequence (vibrate + silence).

### Stratum

A weekly layer in the Depth Index representing accumulated system activity.

### Subsurface

The internal layer where pre-cognitive signals originate.

## T

### Thrum (Anchor Thrum)

Low-frequency vibration emitted by the Anchor during sustained hold.  
Used for grounding.

### Threshold (Void Threshold)

The internal level of subsurface energy at which the Void becomes the recommended pathway.

## V

### Vault

Containment unit for signals.  
Stores without displaying or encouraging review.

### Void

The Purge Engine.  
Dissipates high-intensity subsurface energy through collapse and haptic escalation.

## W

### Well

Metaphorical depth where subsurface energy accumulates.  
Represents the internal reservoir beneath the Bridge.

END OF AEGIS GLOSSARY (V1.0)
