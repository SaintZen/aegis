  - ❌ **Headspace:** No physical exercises (meditation only)
  - ❌ **Sanvello:** No physical exercises (CBT-based, mental techniques)
  - ❌ **Daylio:** No physical exercises (tracking only)
  - ❌ **Wysa:** No physical exercises (AI chat + meditation)
  - ❌ **Insight Timer:** No physical exercises (meditation only)
  - ❌ **BetterSleep:** No physical exercises (sleep-focused)

- "The only anxiety app designed for when you're already in crisis"
- "When breathing isn't enough - physical exercises for immediate relief"
- "Crisis intervention, not just prevention"

**Verdict: SOLID #2 - Rare combination that competitors don't have**

  - ✅ **Headspace:** Has mood tracking, but it's basic

  - ✅ **Wysa:** AI personalization (chat-based, not data-driven)

**Why It's #3:**
