# Private/Discreet Exercises for Anxiety Relief
**Feature:** Exercises You Can Do Anywhere, Anytime
**Total Exercises:** 23 (8 original + 15 new private exercises)

---

## 🎯 **Why Private Exercises Matter**

Many people experience anxiety in:
- **Office/Work** - Can't do jumping jacks at desk
- **Public Spaces** - Need subtle exercises
- **Small Spaces** - Bathroom, bedroom, hallway
- **Quiet Environments** - Library, meeting, classroom

**Solution:** Exercises that are quiet, require minimal space, and look natural.
